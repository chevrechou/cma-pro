How it works
A CMA in four steps
From address to suggested price in under five minutes.
1

Enter the subject property

Address, beds, baths, sqft, year built, and condition. Takes 60 seconds.

2

Review live comparables

The app pulls recently sold properties from Zillow within your ZIP code, matched by size and bedrooms. Tap to include or exclude each one.

3

Apply adjustments

Add dollar adjustments per comp to account for a pool, lot size difference, renovations, or deferred maintenance.

4

Get your price range

Instant suggested price, low/high range, and full market statistics. Save it to your account and attach a client name.

Features
Everything you need at the listing appointment
Built for agents who move fast and need numbers they can defend.
📊

Live Comparable Sales

Pulls from Zillow via RapidAPI — recently sold homes filtered by ZIP, beds, baths, and square footage. Refreshes on demand.

✏️

Per-Comp Adjustments

Add a dollar amount to any comparable to account for a pool, extra garage stall, updated kitchen, or inferior lot. Notes field included.

📈

Market Statistics

Avg and median sale price, price per sqft, average days on market, and list-to-sale ratio — automatically calculated from your selected comps.

🎯

Condition-Weighted Pricing

Set the property condition (Excellent through Poor) and the suggested price automatically adjusts with proven industry multipliers.

💾

Saved Reports

Every CMA you create is saved to your account. Review past reports, pull up the price range for any listing, and track your work over time.

👤

Client Ready

Attach a client name and email to each report. Your agent profile — name, brokerage, license number — can be stored in the app.
